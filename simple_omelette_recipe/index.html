Simple Omlette Reciepe

An omelette is a versatile egg-based dish made from beaten eggs, often folded around various fillings such as cheese, vegetables, or meat, and cooked in butter or oil.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1000 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 995 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter oroil
  • Optional fillings:cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pich of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of wateror milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omlette Once the butter is melyed and bibbling, por in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at all the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over on half of the omlette
  5. Fold and serve As the omlette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot,with additiona salt and pepper if needed.
