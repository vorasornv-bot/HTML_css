How to grow a banana tree

Plant a banana pup or rhizome in a sunny, sheltered spot with rich, well-draining soil. Water the plant frequently to keep the soil consistently moist, especially during hot, dry weather, and fertilize monthly with a high-potassium fertilizer. Remove any unwanted suckers around the base, leaving only one or two, and protect the plant from frost during winter.

How to cook a banana
  1. You can fry sliced bananas in butter and sprinkle them with cinnamon and sugar for a quick caramelized treat.
  2. Mash ripe bananas into batter to make banana bread or pancakes inspired by classics like Joy of Cooking.
  3. You can also grill banana halves in their peels and top them with chocolate and nuts for a warm dessert.
